Recipe for Pico de Gallo

About this recipe

Pico de Gallo (pronounced Pee-ko-the-ga-jo) is a traditional Mexican dresser dish. This recipe is based on the classic, though it is a variation of it. The classic original Pico de Gallo often includes hot pepper and/or bell pepper, which this one does not. You could say this is a light version of traditional Pico de Gallo. If you like tomato, onion, and lemon, you will probably enjoy this recipe.

Ingredients

These are the ingredients you will need to prepare this recipe:
  • Tomatoes.
  • Onions.
  • Lemons (Limes).
  • Optional: Celery.
  • Optional: Avocado.

Recipe Steps

How to prepare the recipe:
  1. Cut two tomatoes into tiny square pieces. Note: Suggest round variety but can use any.
  2. Cut half an onion into tiny square piece. Note: I prefer white onion, but you can use other types.
  3. Cut one or two avocados into square pieces. Note: This is an optional step.
  4. Cut some celery into tiny pieces. Note: This is also an optional step.
  5. Put the chopped fruits and veggies in a bowl, and mix them thoroughly.
  6. Pour one or two spoons of lemon juice and mix again. Note: It is recommended to use green lemons (limes).
